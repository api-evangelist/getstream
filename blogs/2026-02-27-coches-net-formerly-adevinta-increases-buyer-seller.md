coches.net is an expert mobility marketplace and market leader in Spain. At the time of its integration with Stream, coches.net operated as part of Adevinta, alongside other major European marketplace apps, including Milanuncios and Fotocasa. As a high-volume consumer marketplace, coches.net connects buyers and sellers at moments of high intent.
